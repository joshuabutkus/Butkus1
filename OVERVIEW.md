# Alpine Group — Project Overview

**Website:** https://alpineor.com

## What Alpine Group Is

Alpine Group is an owner's representation and project management firm focused on luxury residential construction. Founded in 2016 and operating primarily across the Mountain West, the firm acts on behalf of homeowners to manage custom home builds from first site walk through final closeout.

## What They Do

Alpine Group manages every phase of a custom home project:

- Due diligence and feasibility
- Permitting and approvals
- Contract procurement and vendor selection
- Budgeting and cost control
- Team assembly (architects, builders, trades, consultants)
- Scheduling and timeline protection
- On-site oversight through construction
- Project closeout and handoff

They work as the owner's advocate, coordinating the many parties a custom build requires so the homeowner retains creative control without absorbing the operational load.

## Mission

Alpine Group exists to transform ambitious designs into finished homes while protecting three things the owner cares about most: timeline, budget, and vision. The engagement model is collaborative and detail-led, built around the client experience rather than the construction schedule alone.

## Who They Serve

Busy, affluent homeowners undertaking custom luxury residential projects who want a seasoned professional managing the complexity on their behalf. Clients typically value discretion, expect direct principal involvement, and prefer to delegate logistics so they can stay focused on the creative and lifestyle decisions that matter to them.

## What Makes Them Different

- **Principal oversight on every project.** The team stays small and lean so founders and senior staff remain directly involved, rather than handing work off to junior coordinators.
- **Multi-discipline depth.** The team collectively carries decades of experience as architects, builders, estimators, and project managers, which lets them see issues from every side of the table.
- **Customized engagement.** Scope and involvement are tailored to each client's needs rather than forced into a fixed product.
- **Confidentiality.** Over 100 luxury home projects completed with strict client privacy maintained throughout.
