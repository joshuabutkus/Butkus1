## 1. BD FRAMEWORK

### Signal → Insight → Offer

All BD motion moves through three stages:

```
Signal → Insight → Offer
```

**Signal:** A market signal, relationship event, or intelligence item that indicates a potential opportunity. Signals are not yet qualified — they are inputs to be assessed.

**Insight:** Analysis of the signal against Alpine's positioning, capacity, and strategic fit. This is where Signal becomes directional — either an Offer is warranted or the Signal is filed for future reference.

**Offer:** A tailored, positioned proposal or outreach rooted in the Insight. Not a pitch deck — a specific, relevant move that demonstrates Alpine understands the client's situation.
